## ğŸ“ˆ Model Performance Analysis

### Why Logistic Regression Works Well
1. **Binary classification task** - Perfect for logistic regression
2. **Good feature-target relationship** - Linearly separable to good extent
3. **Interpretable results** - Probability-based predictions
4. **Fast training** - Computational efficiency
5. **ROC AUC of 0.9729** - Excellent discrimination

### Confusion Matrix Interpretation
- **True Positives (63)**: Correctly identified malignant tumors
- **True Negatives (38)**: Correctly identified benign tumors
- **False Positives (4)**: Benign tumors misclassified as malignant
- **False Negatives (9)**: Malignant tumors misclassified as benign

### Precision vs Recall Tradeoff
- **High Precision (0.94)**: When model says malignant, it's usually correct
- **Good Recall (0.88)**: Catches 88% of actual malignant cases
- **Balanced F1-Score (0.91)**: Good overall performance
